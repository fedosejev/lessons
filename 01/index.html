Lesson 1

Art.

Experiment. Produce new experiences. Grow. Test yourself. Understand yourself better. Build your self-awareness.

Don’t be afraid to loose. It’s OK to loose most of the times. It’s part of the game. You will win from time to time. Those wins make the game worth playing.

Don’t be afraid to break relationships. If they can be easily broken, then they are not real, not worth worrying about.

Don’t expect anything from people. They don’t own you anything. You ask. They give you their answer. Don’t worry about the answer. Accept for what it is.

Experiment. Be active. Play offence. It’s the best way to grow.

It’s the best way to experience life.

It’s to live.
